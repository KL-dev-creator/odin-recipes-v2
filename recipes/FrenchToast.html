How to make French toast

Crispy, sweet, fried dessert with simple ingredients

This recipe works with many kinds of bread. Try it with white, wholemeal, or french it is up to you!

This simple recipe only requires seven simple ingredients that you probably have in your house already. So what are you waiting for? let's make make this Sunday morning favorite together.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Vanilla
  • Cinnamon
  • Salt
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Gather all your ingredients
  2. Whisk milk, eggs, vanilla, cinnamon, and salt in a bowl to make a batter
  3. Butter your skillet and heat over medium-high heat
  4. Soak bread with batter making sure to cover both sides
  5. Transfer to skillet and cook until golden on each side
  6. Serve hot and eat
